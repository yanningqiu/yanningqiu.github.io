Assistant Professor at WUR

Yanning Qiu

I am an Assistant Professor in Water Quality and Data Science at the Aquatic Ecology and Water Quality Management Group, Wageningen University & Research (WUR).

My current research focuses on the risk assessment of microplastics on human health and the environment.

I am particularly interested in integrating artificial intelligence into scientific research, including the use of large language models (LLMs) and computer vision (CV) to advance literature synthesis, structured data extraction, and environmental monitoring.

Current interests

My work connects environmental risk assessment with data-intensive research methods. I am especially interested in how AI can support rigorous scientific workflows without sacrificing transparency or domain expertise.

Across projects, I focus on turning scientific literature, observational data, and image-based monitoring into structured evidence that can be used for quantitative analysis and practical environmental decision-making.

Outside research, I enjoy hiking, skiing, birdwatching, and wildlife photography.

  • Risk assessment of microplastics in human and environmental contexts
  • LLM-assisted literature synthesis and QA/QC workflows
  • Structured data extraction from scientific evidence
  • Computer vision for environmental monitoring
